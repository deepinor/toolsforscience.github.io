About

This website locates an article by a citation and redirects you to the online journal in chemistry (and some of biology and physics). It works completely on a client side by JavaScript. You can also download the files and run it in your local folder (Download a zip file).

Supported Publishers

The list of supported journals is here.

  • American Chemical Society
  • Wiley
  • Elsevier
  • Nature Publishing Group
  • American Physical Society
  • Springer
  • Annual Reviews
  • PLoS
  • Cell Press
  • American Institute of Physics
  • Some other journals

Since I haven't tested each individual journal, there is no guarantee that all journals of these publishers are correctly supported.

Parameters

You can specify parameters like this:
http://locator.web.fc2.com/index.html?q=J. Am. Chem. Soc., 2011, 133, 4172â€“4174&redirect=yes
q=[citation text]
Specify a citation text
redirect=[yes/no]
Redirects you to the online journal immediately. (Default: no)
Using these parameters, you can directly jump to the online journal from the address bar in your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari).
